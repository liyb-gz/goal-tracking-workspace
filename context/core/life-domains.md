# Life Domains

Areas of life to track for balanced goal-setting.

## Available Domains

### Health & Energy

Physical fitness, sleep, nutrition, medical care, energy management

### Career & Work

Job performance, projects, professional relationships, workplace goals

### Skills & Learning

New abilities, education, certifications, deliberate practice

### Finances

Savings, investments, spending habits, income growth, financial security

### Relationships

Family, friends, romantic partnership, social connections, community

### Fun & Recreation

Hobbies, travel, entertainment, play, intentional rest

### Environment

Home, workspace, possessions, organization, physical surroundings

### Contribution

Volunteering, mentoring, community impact, giving back

### Spirituality / Mindfulness

Meditation, reflection, meaning-making, inner life

### Creative Expression

Art, writing, music, building, making things

---

## Usage Notes

-   Users select which domains to actively track during onboarding
-   Each domain can be marked as:
    -   **Priority** — More attention, deeper exploration (2-3 goals)
    -   **Active** — Regular check-ins (1 goal)
    -   **Maintenance** — Light touch, occasional review
    -   **Inactive** — Not currently tracking
-   Domains can be added, removed, or reprioritized anytime
-   Custom domains are welcome — users may have life areas not listed here
